Rooted In Place

Rooted in Place is an e-commerce site that represents a fictional plant store. A modern yet minimalistic design was the inspiration behind the creation of this website. A clean and simple design appealed to me, so I created a simple silhouette with a very subtle color introduction. It was important to me to choose a color palette that complimented one another well without being overpowering.
